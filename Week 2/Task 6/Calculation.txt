RISK ASSESSMENT CALCULATION (ALE)

Asset Value (AV) = 100,000
Exposure Factor (EF) = 0.5
Annual Rate of Occurrence (ARO) = 2

Step 1: Calculate Single Loss Expectancy (SLE)
SLE = AV × EF
SLE = 100,000 × 0.5 = 50,000

Step 2: Calculate Annual Loss Expectancy (ALE)
ALE = SLE × ARO
ALE = 50,000 × 2 = 100,000

Final Result:
The Annual Loss Expectancy (ALE) is 100,000.

Interpretation:
The organization can expect an estimated annual loss of 100,000 due to the identified risk. This helps prioritize security controls and mitigation strategies.
