A risk assessment was performed using the Annual Loss Expectancy (ALE) model. The asset value was estimated at 100,000 with an exposure factor of 0.5, resulting in a single loss expectancy (SLE) of 50,000. The annual rate of occurrence (ARO) was assumed to be 2. Based on these values, the ALE was calculated as 100,000. This indicates that the expected annual financial loss due to the identified risk is 100,000. The calculation helps organizations understand potential impact and prioritize security controls to reduce risk.
